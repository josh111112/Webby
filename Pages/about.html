My Journey Behind the Lens

Photography has been part of my life as long as I can remember. I grew up hiking all the time and taking as many photos as I could. A couple years ago I started photographing people, that eventually turned into weddings, and I've found I really enjoy it.

Outside of photos, I'm a huge hobbyist. I love to build things, I coded this entire website from scratch, and I have lots of DIY projects. I could talk about my hobbies all day but that's not why you're here.
I am a nature and animal person, so if your dog is in the wedding party I am beyond excited.
